About Us

About Us

Nathan C Car-Dealership Company Limited is a leading car dealership company located in Zambia with its head office in the Copperbelt city of Kitwe.

We are specialized in the import and sales of brand new and used motor vehicles from South Africa. We have a long standing relationship with reputable exporters and importers such as Hello Logistics Zambia and UVS South Africa which is one of the biggest exporters in Africa. Our other partners include banks and insurance companies with whom we have a long standing relationship with. These institutions ensure that all invoices and orders are insured to avoid any lapse as we provide you with only the best experience.
We deliver quality vehicles to your door step so that you can concentrate on your business.

Our Vision

To be the top leading Car Dealership in Zambia with emphasis on customer service, retention and satisfactory experience.

Our Mission

To achieve the reputation of a quality, high standard & reliable motor vehicle supplier & service provider company.
